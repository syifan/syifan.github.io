About

Yifan Sun is a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at Northeastern University. His research interest lies in performance modeling for CPU and GPU systems, GPU micro-architecture design, and high-performance computing. He received his Master of Science (M.S.) degree from the Department of Electrical Engineering of University at Buffalo in 2013 and received his Bachelor of Science (B.S.) degree from the Department of Electronic Science and Technology of Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2007.
