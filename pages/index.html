About

Yifan Sun is a Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at Northeastern University. He works under the advisory of Dr. David Kaeli in the Northeastern University Computer Architecture (NUCAR) Group. His research interest lies in performance modeling for CPU and GPU systems, GPU micro-architecture design, and high-performance computing. He received Master of Science (M.S.) degree from the Department of Electrical Engineering of University at Buffalo in 2013 and received Bachelor of Science (B.S.) degree from the Department of Electronic Science and Technology of Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2007.
